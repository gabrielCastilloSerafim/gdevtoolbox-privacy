Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

G Dev Toolbox does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Ever.

This privacy policy describes how G Dev Toolbox ("the app") handles information on your device. The app is developed and maintained by Gabriel Castillo as an independent project.

Data Collection

G Dev Toolbox collects no data of any kind. Specifically:

  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No personal information
  • No usage statistics
  • No network requests are made by the app

All functionality runs entirely on your device. Nothing leaves your Mac.

System Permissions

The app requests the following macOS permissions. All processing happens locally on your device and the data is never stored or transmitted.

Accessibility

Required to track your cursor position globally across all applications. This is used exclusively to position the overlay tools (Crosshair, Loupe, Ruler, Angle, Color Picker) in real time.

Screen Recording

Required by macOS for ScreenCaptureKit, which the Loupe magnifier and Color Picker use to capture the pixels under your cursor. Screenshots are processed in memory and immediately discarded — nothing is saved to disk.

User Preferences

The app saves two preferences to your local device using macOS standard storage (NSUserDefaults): your selected zoom level for the Loupe tool and your preferred ruler unit. These values never leave your Mac.

Third Parties

G Dev Toolbox does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

The optional "Buy Me a Coffee" link in the menu opens buymeacoffee.com in your browser. Any interaction with that page is governed by Buy Me a Coffee's own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

G Dev Toolbox does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
